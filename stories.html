Webstory Writing

These are some of the webstories I have written for KOMU 8 News.

Missouri Humane Society rescuing animals stranded in flooded areas

June 4, 2019

After days of rain and stormy weather, river levels were rising and communities were flooded. The Missouri Humane Society took boats and trucks to those areas to save stranded animals.

Areas in Chariton County evacuating due to levee break

May 30, 2019

One man in the community got in his private plane and took to the skies to take pictures and video of the flooding. I reached out to him over Facebook messenger to talk to him about what he saw.

One confirmed dead in southeast Columbia house fire

May 29, 2019

Like the story above, being a digital producer requires keeping an eye on social media. I saw this on the Columbia Fire Department's Twitter page, and we were able to send a reporter to the scene before the fire was put out.

Parson says Planned Parenthood has itself to blame for license issues

May 28, 2019

After a dispute between Planned Parenthood and the state of Missouri over the renewal of a St. Louis clinic's abortion license, Missouri Gov. Mike Parson held a press conference addressing the dispute.

MSHP finds body of missing swimmer at Lake of the Ozarks

May 27, 2019

After an overnight search, the Missouri State Highway Patrol found the body of a man who went missing while swimming. For Troop F, this was the first boating fatality of 2019.

Winds reached up to 160 mph in Jefferson City tornado

May 23, 2019

This is a story combining a lot of KOMU's coverage of the tornado that wrecked through Jefferson City in late May 2019.
